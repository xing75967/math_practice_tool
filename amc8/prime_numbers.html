Prime Numbers (Under 100)

Only need to check primes ≤ √100 = 10 → 2, 3, 5, 7

Divisibility Rules

  • Divisible by 2: Last digit is even (0,2,4,6,8)
  • Divisible by 3: Sum of digits is divisible by 3
  • Divisible by 4: Last two digits form a number divisible by 4
  • Divisible by 5: Ends in 0 or 5
  • Divisible by 6: Divisible by both 2 and 3
  • Divisible by 7: Double last digit and subtract from rest (advanced)
  • Divisible by 8: Last three digits divisible by 8
  • Divisible by 9: Sum of digits divisible by 9
  • Divisible by 10: Ends in 0
