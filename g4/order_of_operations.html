➕➖✖➗ Mixed Operations
(Order of Operations)


📘 Order of Operations

1️⃣ Multiplication and Division first

× and ÷ are calculated from left to right.

2️⃣ Then Addition and Subtraction

+ and − are calculated from left to right.

3️⃣ Step-by-step rule

Always solve one operation at a time, from left to right.

中文：先乘除后加减，同级从左到右计算
